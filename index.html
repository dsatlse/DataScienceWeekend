The LSESU Data Science Weekend is our inaugural flagship event. Part Conference and part Hackathon.

The event will showcase interesting career areas in Data Science: Finance and Consulting.
Students will also be tasked to develop and showcase their skills by solving a Data Science challenge.

27-28th February 2021, virtually on Zoom
